Important Links

  • ResearchGate

    Faculty can share their research publications, track citations, and collaborate with peers on research projects.

  • Google Scholar

    A platform for faculty to track their published articles, h-index, and i10-index for academic performance evaluation.

  • National Digital Library of India (NDLI)

    Access a wide range of academic resources, e-books, and journals for research and teaching purposes.

  • AICTE Faculty Development Portal

    Facilitates registration for faculty development programs (FDPs), workshops, and training organized by AICTE.

  • Scopus

    A comprehensive database to evaluate faculty's research impact, including citations and publication metrics.

  • Turnitin

    Helps in checking plagiarism in academic and research papers, ensuring originality in scholarly work.

  • Moodle or Learning Management System (LMS)

    Faculty can manage their courses, upload assignments, and engage with students in a virtual learning environment.

  • Faculty Professional Development Dashboard

    A dedicated portal to track and manage continuous professional development (CPD) activities.

  • Institution's Official Website

    Faculty can access official announcements, HR policies, and important updates related to their roles.

  • UGC Academic Staff College (HRDC)

    Supports faculty in registering for orientation and refresher courses approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC).
